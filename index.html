SEMINAR PROGRAM

Saturday, November 16 and Sunday, November 17

 

Two days of exciting sit-down seminars with Cigar Aficionado's editors and some of the world's best cigarmakers, discussing intriguing tobacco-related topics.

Top 3 Cigars

The tradition of sharing the top cigars of the year as presented by their makers continues with an extraordinary trio:

• No. 1: E.P. Carrillo Encore Majestic

• No. 2: My Father La Opulencia Toro

• No. 3: The Wise Man Maduro Robusto

 

Power Tobacco

Ligero: the most powerful tobacco varietal. Three noteworthy tobacco men discuss growing and blending this extraordinary varietal.

 

Fighting the FDA

Cigarmakers and retailers discuss ways to help in the fight against FDA regulations and restrictions of premium cigars.

Investing in Cuban Cigars

Learn how to build a Cuban cigar collection and invest in cigars that appreciate in value.

 

Women in the Cigar Industry

A discussion by prominent women in the cigar business.

 

Roll Your Own

Learn to roll your own cigar like a pro and then enjoy the fruits of your labor!

 

Cigars & Spirits

The only thing better than a great cigar is a glass of something delicious to go with it.

FAQ

What time do the Big Smoke Evenings start on Friday and Saturday nights?

5:30 PM: VIP Entry
6:30 PM: General Admission Entry
9:30 PM: Event Ends
The Big Smoke Evening events will take place in the Mirage Event Center

 

Can I smoke at the event?

Yes, cigars can be enjoyed in the ballrooms at the Mirage Event Center.

 

Will there be food at the Big Smoke Evenings?

Yes, there will be a delicious buffet with hot and cold dishes for you to enjoy.

 

Can I purchase a ticket to attend a Seminar?

Seminars are included in the weekend package. Individual seminar tickets are not available.

 

Dress Code?

Be casual. Have fun!

 

Additional Questions?

Send us an e-mail at events@mshanken.com. We will be happy to answer them!
