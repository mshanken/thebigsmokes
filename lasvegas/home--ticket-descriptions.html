FAQ

What time do the Big Smoke Evenings start on Friday and Saturday nights?

5:30 PM: VIP Entry
6:30 PM: General Admission Entry
9:30 PM: Event Ends
The Big Smoke Evening events will take place in the Mirage Event Center

 

Can I smoke at the event?

Yes, cigars can be enjoyed in the ballrooms at the Mirage Event Center.

 

Will there be food at the Big Smoke Evenings?

Yes, there will be a delicious selection of food offered in a safe and healthy manner.

 

Can I purchase a ticket to attend a Seminar?

Seminars are included in the weekend package. Individual seminar tickets are not available.

 

Dress Code?

Be casual. Enjoy the night!

 

What is the refund policy?

A full refund is available for all tickets until October 20, 2020. After that time, no refunds will be issued. If the event is cancelled, all ticket holders will have the option to obtain a full refund.

 

What safety precautions will The Big Smoke and The Mirage Casino & Hotel be taking due to COVID-19 Concerns?

We are working closely with The Mirage (MGM) and internally to implement health and safety protocols that will ensure a safe and enjoyable experience. We will be sharing an overview of those protocols once they are finalized. Stay tuned!

 

Additional Questions?

Send us an e-mail at events@mshanken.com. We will be happy to answer them!
